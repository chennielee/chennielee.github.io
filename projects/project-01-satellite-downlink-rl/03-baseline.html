03 — Baselines & Metrics

Baseline policies (no RL)

  1. Best Available Link: choose GS with max link_quality among available ones
  2. Random Available: random choice among available GSs
  3. Always-0: always pick GS 0 (sanity check)

Metrics to log every episode

  • Total reward
  • Total sent data (throughput)
  • Total energy used
  • Failure count (unavailable selections)

Output format (simple)

Write a CSV per run, e.g.:

episode,total_reward,total_sent,total_energy,fails
0,12.3,18.0,5.0,2
1,10.8,16.5,4.8,1
