01 — Overview & Research Question

One-liner

I’m building a toy satellite downlink environment and training RL agents (baseline → DQN → hierarchical) to study throughput vs energy trade-offs.

Why this project

  • I want a hands-on way to understand RL without starting from heavy math.
  • This environment is small enough to iterate fast and learn from failures.

Weekly plan (initial)

  1. Week 1: environment + baseline + metrics
  2. Week 2: DQN runs + learning curve
  3. Week 3: energy-aware reward (λ sweep)
  4. Week 4: failure case logging (episodes where reward collapses)
